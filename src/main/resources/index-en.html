But I don't enter my data anywhere, how do they get into my account?

WWhen you log into Discord, whether in the client, browser app, or elsewhere, your token is stored on your device. This token is used to perform actions like writing messages on Discord. In a way, the token is a password to perform actions on Discord.
These scam sites connect to Discord servers from your computer in order to read the data stored on your computer that is related to Discord. During this process, they also find your token and it is transmitted to the site operators. They then use it to distribute such links or do other things with it. What can I do about it?

This is relatively simple. Do not click on links where you are not 120% sure that you can trust them. The best protection against viruses or hackers is the human brain. As long as people on the Internet do not become more suspicious, such links will continue to exist, because attackers know that the weakest point of a system is the user.

Detect Discord-Scam-Links: At the domain

For links, you can pay attention to the domain. Providers always have their own domains. For Discord, for example, it is discord.com or discordapp.com. Attackers try to choose domains that are as similar as possible by making small changes to the spelling, as this is easily overlooked. However, if you take a closer look at the domain, you will notice this very quickly.
